AM GOLF — INTERACTIVE DEMO
===========================

HOW TO VIEW:
Just double-click "index.html" — it opens in your web browser.
No install, no server needed.

WHAT THIS IS:
A clickable prototype of the AM Golf store experience — click products,
pick a size, add to cart, adjust quantities, see the cart drawer update.

WHAT THIS IS NOT:
This is a standalone demo, not connected to Shopify. The cart is just
local JavaScript in your browser — it does not process real orders,
real inventory, or real payments. It's for previewing the UX flow only.

NEXT STEP:
To make this work on your real Shopify store, the cart logic needs to
be rewired to Shopify's Cart AJAX API (/cart/add.js, /cart/change.js)
and connected to real product/variant IDs once you've uploaded your
products in Shopify Admin.
